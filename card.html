Sand cat

Sand cats don't need to drink water at all! They will instead obtain any moisture they need from their prey.

  • Scientific name: Felis margarita
  • Average mass: 2.8 kg
  • Average livespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: sandy and stony deserts

The sand cat, also known as the sand dune cat, is a small wild cat. With its sandy to light grey fur, it is well camouflaged in a desert environment. Its head-and-body length ranges from 39–52 cm with a 23–31 cm long tail
